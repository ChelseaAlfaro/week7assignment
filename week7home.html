About

Robin Hobb's Realm of the Elderlings is a high fantasty epic of 16 novels that follow multliple characters in several different regions of the world that Hobb created.
The series is broken into 5 separate series that intersect with each other at various points in the timelines. Throughout the series Hobb explores various themes including
mental health, trauma, societal structres and biases, addiction, sacrifice and isolation. Hobb's Realm of the Elderlings is not just for fantasy lovers as it delves deep into
the personal development of her main characters that allows readers to not just grow to love the characteres but to explore their own psyche as it is reflected in the lives of the characters.
